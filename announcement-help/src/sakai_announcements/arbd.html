Reading announcements

You can read announcements from within each worksite to which you belong. Enter a site, and from the menubar, click Announcements. To read an announcement, click its title.

From My Workspace, you can view a consolidated list of announcements from all your sites. Enter My Workspace, and from the menubar click Announcements. To read an announcement, click its title.

You can sort the announcements list in the following ways:

  • To arrange the announcements alphabetically by title, click Subject. Click Subject again to reverse the order.

  • To arrange the announcements alphabetically by the author's last name, click From. Click From again to reverse the order.

  • In My Workspace, to arrange the announcements alphabetically by the originating site, click Site. Click Site again to reverse the order.

  • In a course or project site, to arrange the announcements alphabetically by audience, click For. Click For again to reverse the order.

  • To arrange the announcements chronologically, click Date. Click Date again to reverse the order.
